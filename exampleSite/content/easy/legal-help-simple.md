Sometimes people have problems with their home or their job.

It can be hard to know what to do.

You do not have to deal with this alone.

## Free legal help

There are volunteer lawyers who can help you.

They give free advice.

You can talk to them for 20 minutes.

## What kind of problems?

They can help with housing problems.

For example: if your landlord is not making repairs.

Or if you have been treated unfairly at work.

## How to get an appointment

The clinic is open once a month.

You can book a free slot.

Ask at the community centre for more information.
