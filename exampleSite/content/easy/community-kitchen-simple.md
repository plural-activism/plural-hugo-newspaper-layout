There is a community kitchen in our neighbourhood.

The kitchen is open every Saturday and Sunday.

## What happens there?

People come together to cook food.

They share the food with each other.

Everyone is welcome.

## How can you help?

You can help cook the food.

You can help give out the food.

You can help clean up afterwards.

You do not need to be an expert.

Everyone can learn together.

## Where is it?

The kitchen is at the community centre.

Look for the sign on the door.

Come any time between 10 in the morning and 2 in the afternoon.
